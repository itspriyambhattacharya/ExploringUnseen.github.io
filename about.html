About Me

Nikhil Sharma is a famous Moto Vlogger from India who has a YouTube channel name “Mumbiker Nikhil” which has over 2.2 Million subscribers. Mumbiker Nikhil is a much-known name among the bikers and moto vloggers around the world.
Nikhil Sharma was born on November 4, 1991 ( 38 years old as of 2019) in Mumbai to Anil Kumar Sharma and his mother. He was interested in videography from his childhood. Nikhil has a younger sister who works for Red Chillies Entertainment as a graphics designer. Nikhil was graduating in hotel management from the Rizvi Institute of Studies and Research in Mumbai, but for some reason, he did not complete it. In 2007, he got a chance to work as a flight attendant in Qatar Airways and was living in Doha, Qatar for 6 years before coming back to Mumbai and tried his luck in acting for his career but sadly he couldn’t do much in acting. After that, In 2012 December his father died and he has to look after his father business. He started his YouTube channel “Mumbiker Nikkhil” on 3rd July 2013 and now planning to be a full-time YouTuber.

Career / Awards & Achievements :-
In July 2013, Mumbiker Nikhil began sharing videos in the form of vlogs and, as he was passionate about motorcycles rides bikes since the age of 15, he combined them both and began to publish the motovlog on YouTube. First of all, he didn’t get the fame right away as he continued to publish quality content and amazing videos of his bike rides to multiple destinations. His hard work and dedication pay off and today he is one of the famous YouTuber from India. His vlog about Leh Ladakh trip was the turning point of his YouTubing career. Because of this trip, he has not only become famous, he gained a huge number of subscribers and also during this trip, he also met Debashish Ghosh who is currently on a world ride in his BMW bike. On August 26, 2016, he surpassed 100,000 subscribers on YouTube and things began to change. He gained a lot of followers and fans around the world. His videos got appreciated and got renowned for his YouTube channels name “Mumbiker Nikhil”. In 2017, he went for K2K (Kanyakumari to Kashmir) ride on his KTM Duke 390 to meet his fans all over the country. Nikhil has collabed with many famous Indian YouTuber which includes Bhuvan Bam, Ashish Chanchlani, Be Younick, Technical Guruji & Prajakta Koli. In 2017, Nikhil was invited to the YouTube Fanfest in Mumbai. He performed an act on the stage with a Harley Davidson which he borrowed it from his friend Debashish Ghosh. It helped Nikhil to gain more subscribers and on March 16, 2018, he achieved 1 Million subscribers on his YouTube Channel. Till date Nikhil as posted 616 videos in his channel and has over 18 Million Views.

Nikhil Sharma’s main source of income comes from comes from YouTube Videos & Sponsorship from nds. Currently, his net worth is unknown.
